Blinchiki

  • Time:30 minutes
  • Serves:5
Ingredients Quantity
Water 500 ml
Flour 500 g
Sugar 100 g
Milk 500 ml
Eggs 2

Step 1: Let's prepare all the ingredients. It's best if they heat up, as this will help them blend better. Therefore, it's best to remove the eggs (3) and milk (500 ml) from the refrigerator in advance. For the oil (30 g or 2 tablespoons), you can use either vegetable oil, refined (odorless), or butter. Butter gives the pancakes a richer brown color and a creamier flavor. If using butter, melt it and let it cool.

Step 2:Heat some of the milk, add salt, and mix thoroughly with the beaten eggs and sugar. Gradually add flour to the mixture, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Add a little baking soda, salt, and butter. Stir. Add the remaining milk and whisk. The batter should be moderately runny. Heat the frying pan. Fry the pancake on both sides until golden brown.
